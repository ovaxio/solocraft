# Product validation framework — solo founder

Adapted to profile: INTP, Learner, solo dev, aversion to prospecting,
bias toward useful systems.

## Quick saas-builder filter (5 questions)

1. Who pays? Individual, SMB, large enterprise, regulated profession?
   -> Prefer SMB or liberal profession: short cycle, identifiable budget

2. Exact pain? Reformulate in one sentence without jargon.
   -> If you can't in one sentence, the problem isn't clear enough

3. Paid alternatives? Competitors are validation, not an obstacle.
   -> 0 competitors = non-existent market or poorly defined problem

4. Obvious distribution? How to reach the first 10 customers?
   -> Without an obvious channel for a solo without a sales team, it's a critical risk

5. Buildable solo in 4-6 weeks? Happy path only, no multiple roles.
   -> If not, break it down until yes

## Real validation signals (by strength)

1. Someone agrees to pay now -> GO
2. Someone signs a letter of intent -> GO with conditions
3. Strong repeated verbal interest -> validate quickly
4. "That's a good idea" -> worthless

## Profile anti-patterns to flag

- Building a complex feature before having 1 customer
- Pricing too low due to impostor syndrome
- No distribution channel identified
- Feature that requires intensive cold prospecting
